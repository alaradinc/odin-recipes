Lasagna Recipe

Grandma's traditional Lasagna!

Go back to Home Page

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • dough / flat pasta
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • yogurt

Cooking Steps

  1. prep the meat by slightly cooking it
  2. place the pasta in the pan
  3. pour the meat over it
  4. layer the pasta
  5. place the pan in the oven
  6. cook until slightly bronze
